Bananas or Oranges?

Fact Accordion!

First

Bananas help you digest food easier! This is due to the high amount of Fiber bananas contain.

Second

Bananas can give you more energy, which is really helpful when you're about to workout!

Third

Bananas have Tryptophan which your body uses to create Serotonin. Proper Serotonin levels help your mood, regulates stress, and regulates your sleep schedule as well!
